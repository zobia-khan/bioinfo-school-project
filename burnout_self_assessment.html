Your Body

Physical tension tells us how hard your nervous system is working. Heart Rate Variability (HRV) is one of the most reliable biomarkers of burnout — often rising before you consciously feel it.

📊 Contributes 30% to your score

Your Sleep

Sleep is when your brain flushes stress proteins and consolidates memory. Poor sleep both causes burnout and makes every other symptom worse.

📊 Contributes 15% to your score
